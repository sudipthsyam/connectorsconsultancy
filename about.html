About us

Connectors Consultancy is a distinguished service firm headquartered in Alappuzha, Kerala, committed to providing a comprehensive range of professional services in the fields of land acquisition, legal consultancy, and social impact studies. Founded by a dedicated team of seasoned professionals, our company draws upon decades of collective experience from diverse backgrounds, including Advocates, Engineers, Social Workers, and retired Service Pensioners, with a special focus on individuals well-versed in Land Revenue Department matters.
